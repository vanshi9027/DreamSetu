# DreamSetu AI Workflow

## Purpose

The AI Workflow defines how Artificial Intelligence powers DreamSetu.

Instead of replacing teachers or providing direct answers, AI acts as an intelligent learning companion that guides students, forms balanced teams, personalizes learning, and tracks continuous growth.

---

# AI Philosophy

DreamSetu believes that AI should **guide learning, not replace learning.**

Our AI focuses on:

- Understanding each learner
- Creating collaborative Setu Pods
- Assigning personalized tasks
- Supporting teamwork
- Tracking growth
- Encouraging confidence

# Step 1 — AI Skill Assessment

## Purpose

Understand the student's current ability.

### Assessment Includes

- Technical Questions
- Logical Reasoning
- Communication
- Project Experience
- Learning Goals

---

## AI Output

The AI predicts:

- Current Skill Level
- Strong Areas
- Weak Areas
- Learning Speed
- Recommended Domain

🎯 Expert

# Step 3 — AI Creates Setu Pods ⭐

This is DreamSetu's core innovation.

Instead of students searching for teammates,

AI automatically creates balanced learning teams.

Each Setu Pod contains:

🌱 Beginner

🚀 Intermediate

🎯 Expert

AI considers:

- Skill Level
- Learning Goals
- Domain
- Language
- Weekly Availability
- Collaboration History

Goal

Create teams where everyone learns while contributing.

---

# Step 4 — AI Project Recommendation

Based on the team's skill level,

AI recommends suitable projects.

Example

Beginner Pod

Portfolio Website

Intermediate Pod

Weather Dashboard

Advanced Pod

Full Stack Chat Application

Projects become more challenging as students improve.

---

# Step 5 — AI Task Distribution ⭐

AI divides one project into role-based tasks.

Example

Project

Portfolio Website

🌱 Beginner

- HTML Structure
- Buttons
- Images
- Basic CSS

🚀 Intermediate

- Responsive Design
- JavaScript
- Animations

🎯 Expert

- Architecture
- Optimization
- Code Review
- Mentorship

Every student contributes according to their current skill level.

---

# Step 6 — AI Mentor

AI becomes a learning companion.

Instead of giving direct solutions,

AI provides:

- Hints
- Concept Explanations
- Resource Recommendations
- Best Practices
- Debugging Guidance

AI encourages independent thinking.

---

# Step 7 — AI Collaboration Analysis

The AI observes healthy collaboration.

Examples

- Who helped teammates?
- Who asked meaningful questions?
- Who reviewed code?
- Who completed assigned tasks?
- Who actively participated?

This creates a Contribution Score.

---

# Step 8 — AI Progress Tracking

AI continuously monitors:

Technical Skills

- Coding
- Problem Solving
- Project Completion

Soft Skills

- Communication
- Teamwork
- Leadership
- Confidence

Students receive personalized improvement suggestions.

---

# Step 10 — Next Learning Path
