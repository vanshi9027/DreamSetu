# User Personas

---

# Persona 1: Beginner Student 🌱

## Profile

- Age: 16–22
- Education: School / College Student
- Skill Level: Beginner
- Goal: Learn coding or any professional skill by building real projects with guidance.

## Pain Points

- Afraid to ask questions because of fear of judgment.
- Doesn't know where to begin.
- Watches many tutorials but cannot build projects independently.
- Lacks confidence.
- Feels isolated while learning.
- Doesn't have teammates to learn with.

## Needs

- A safe and supportive environment.
- Step-by-step guidance.
- Practical learning through real projects.
- Encouragement instead of comparison.
- Mentors and teammates who can help.

## Role Inside Setu Pod

- Completes beginner-friendly tasks.
- Asks questions freely.
- Learns by observing teammates.
- Builds confidence through contribution.

## How DreamSetu Helps

- AI Skill Assessment identifies current level.
- AI places the student into a balanced Setu Pod.
- Receives beginner-level project tasks.
- AI Mentor provides hints and resources.
- Learns directly from Intermediate and Expert teammates.
- Confidence and skill progress are tracked automatically.

# Persona 2: Intermediate Learner 🚀

## Profile

- Age: 18–25
- Education: College Student
- Skill Level: Intermediate
- Goal: Become industry-ready by building projects and improving communication skills.

## Pain Points

- Doesn't know how to move from tutorials to real projects.
- Struggles with consistency.
- Wants practical experience.
- Rarely gets opportunities to explain concepts.
- Needs portfolio-worthy projects.

## Needs

- Team collaboration.
- Real project experience.
- Leadership opportunities.
- Peer discussions.
- Feedback from experienced developers.

## Role Inside Setu Pod

- Completes medium-complexity tasks.
- Explains concepts to beginners.
- Collaborates with experts.
- Connects different parts of the project.

## How DreamSetu Helps

- Receives intermediate-level project tasks.
- AI recommends learning resources.
- Improves communication through mentoring beginners.
- Builds portfolio through collaborative projects.
- Tracks technical and collaboration growth.

# Persona 3: Expert Student / Mentor 🎯

## Profile

- Age: 20–30
- Education: College Student / Professional
- Skill Level: Advanced

## Goal

Guide others while improving leadership, mentoring, and software engineering skills.

## Pain Points

- No structured mentoring platform.
- Contributions often go unnoticed.
- Limited opportunities to lead project teams.
- Wants meaningful impact on the learning community.

## Needs

- Leadership opportunities.
- Mentor recognition.
- Networking.
- Strong technical challenges.
- Community reputation.

## Role Inside Setu Pod

- Reviews code.
- Guides teammates.
- Suggests best practices.
- Refines project architecture.
- Helps resolve blockers.

## How DreamSetu Helps

- AI assigns mentor role.
- Mentor Dashboard.
- Reputation and contribution system.
- Leadership achievements.
- Public mentor profile.

# Persona 4: Rural & Underserved Student 🌍

## Profile

- Lives in rural or underserved communities.
- Limited access to mentors.
- Limited exposure to industry opportunities.

## Pain Points

- Lack of guidance.
- Poor networking.
- Low confidence.
- Language barriers.
- Fewer opportunities to collaborate.

## Needs

- Equal opportunities.
- Mentorship.
- Community support.
- Local language assistance.
- Career awareness.

## How DreamSetu Helps

- AI matches students with suitable Setu Pods.
- Multilingual AI support.
- Personalized learning guidance.
- Equal access to mentors.
- Real project collaboration regardless of location.
